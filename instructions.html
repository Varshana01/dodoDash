Game Instructions

In "Dodo Dash," you’ll embark on an exhilarating adventure to save the last remaining dodo from the clutches of the notorious Dutchman! As you navigate through vibrant landscapes, your mission is to help this charming bird escape by collecting delicious fruits scattered along the way. Use the arrow keys to guide the dodo—press the left arrow to move left, the right arrow to dash forward, and the up arrow to leap into the air! Every fruit you collect adds to your score, bringing you one step closer to a triumphant escape. Are you ready to take flight and help the dodo thrive?

Controls

← Move Left
→ Move Right
↑ Jump
